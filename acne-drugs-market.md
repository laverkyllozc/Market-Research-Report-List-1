<p><p>The acne drugs market is divided into two primary types: injection acne drugs and external acne drugs. External acne drugs encompass topical formulations such as gels, creams, and lotions containing active ingredients like benzoyl peroxide, retinoids, and antibiotics, used to treat mild to moderate acne by targeting bacteria and exfoliating the skin to prevent clogged pores.</p></p>
