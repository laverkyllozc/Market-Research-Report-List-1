<p><p>The automatic opening laparoscopic retrieval bag market consists of two main types: detachable and non-detachable retrieval bags. Detachable retrieval bags allow for easy separation from the laparoscopic instrument after use, facilitating efficient specimen retrieval and minimizing contamination. In contrast, non-detachable retrieval bags remain attached to the instrument during the procedure, providing a secure method for capturing and extracting tissue without the risk of losing it. Both types aim to enhance surgical efficiency and patient safety in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
