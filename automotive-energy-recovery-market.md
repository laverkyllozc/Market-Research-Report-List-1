<p><p>The automotive energy recovery market encompasses technologies that enhance vehicle efficiency by reclaiming energy. The regenerative braking system captures kinetic energy during braking, converting it into electrical energy for reuse. Turbochargers improve engine efficiency by using exhaust gases to increase air intake, boosting power without additional fuel consumption. Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) reduces nitrogen oxide emissions by redirecting exhaust gases back into the engine, improving combustion efficiency and lowering pollution. Together, these technologies support more sustainable automotive performance.</p></p>
