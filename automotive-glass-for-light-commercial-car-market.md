<p><p>The automotive glass market for light commercial vehicles comprises three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength and safety, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. Laminated glass consists of interlayered layers, providing enhanced durability and sound insulation while preventing shattering. The "others" category may include specialized glasses like tinted or reflective options, catering to specific design and performance needs. Together, these types enhance vehicle safety, aesthetics, and overall functionality.</p></p>
