<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering (EPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Electronic & Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power steering systems market includes three primary types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), and Electronic & Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS utilizes electric motors for steering assistance, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing weight. HPS employs hydraulic fluid pressure for steering support, providing a traditional feel and strong feedback. EHPS combines both systems, offering the advantages of hydraulic response and electric control, optimizing efficiency and performance. Together, these systems cater to diverse automotive needs and preferences.</p></p>
