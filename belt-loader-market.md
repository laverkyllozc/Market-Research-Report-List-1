<p><p>The Belt Loader market is primarily divided into two types: self-propelled belt loaders and towed belt loaders. Self-propelled belt loaders are autonomous units equipped with their own engines, offering enhanced mobility and operational efficiency for loading and unloading cargo at airports or warehouses. In contrast, towed belt loaders require a towing vehicle for operation and are generally more cost-effective, suitable for less frequent tasks or facilities with limited space.
