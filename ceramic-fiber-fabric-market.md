<p><ul><li>Ceramic Oxide Fiber</li><li>Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Fiber Fabric market is divided into two main types: Ceramic Oxide Fiber and Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber. Ceramic Oxide Fibers typically include materials like alumina and silica, known for their thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, Ceramic Non-Oxide Fibers, like silicon carbide, offer superior mechanical properties and resistance to oxidation, often used in advanced aerospace and industrial environments. Both types play critical roles in insulation and heat management across various industries.</p></p>
