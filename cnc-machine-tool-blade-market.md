<p><p>The CNC machine tool blade market encompasses various types, primarily turning, drilling, and milling tool blades. Turning tool blades are designed for shaping materials by rotating them against a stationary cutting tool. Drilling tool blades create holes in various materials using rotary motion and are essential for precise bore creation. Milling tool blades utilize rotating cutting edges to remove material from workpieces, enabling complex shapes and surfaces. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and precision in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
