<p><p>The compressed fibreboard market encompasses various types, primarily Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) and Hardboard. MDF is created from wood fibers combined with adhesives under heat and pressure, resulting in a versatile, smooth surface suitable for furniture and cabinetry. Hardboard, on the other hand, is denser and made from compressed wood fibers, offering greater durability and strength, often used for wall panels and flooring. Both types cater to diverse applications, driven by their unique properties and manufacturing techniques.</p></p>
