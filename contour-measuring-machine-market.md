<p><ul><li>Contact Measuring Machine</li><li>Non-contact Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contour measuring machine market is divided into two main types: contact measuring machines and non-contact measuring machines. Contact measuring machines utilize physical probes to touch and measure the surface profiles of a workpiece, ensuring precision in detailed contour analysis. In contrast, non-contact measuring machines employ laser, optical, or other digital methods to capture surface features without physical contact, enhancing measurement speed and preserving delicate surfaces. Both types are essential for quality control in various manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
