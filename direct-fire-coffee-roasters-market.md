<p><strong>The Direct-Fire Coffee Roasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Drum Roasters</li><li>Perforated Drum Roasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-fire coffee roasters primarily include solid drum roasters and perforated drum roasters, each catering to distinct market needs. Solid drum roasters utilize a solid metal drum that retains more heat, enabling robust flavor profiles and consistent roasting. In contrast, perforated drum roasters feature holes that promote airflow, allowing for more even heat distribution and quicker roasting times. Both types appeal to artisan coffee shops and commercial roasters, each prioritizing different aspects of flavor development and operational efficiency.</p></p>
