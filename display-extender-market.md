<p><p>The Display Extender Market is categorized into two main types based on distance capabilities: Short-Range Extenders and Long-Range Extenders. Short-Range Extenders, typically covering distances up to 50 meters, are ideal for home or office environments where close proximity connection to displays is required. In contrast, Long-Range Extenders exceed 50 meters, suitable for larger spaces like auditoriums or industrial settings, enabling connections over greater distances while maintaining video quality. Both types cater to diverse user needs in various applications.</p></p>
