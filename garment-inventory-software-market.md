<p><p>Garment inventory software is available primarily in two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access their inventory data from anywhere with an internet connection, ensuring flexibility and scalability. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on individual computers or servers within the organization, offering greater control over data security but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Both types serve to streamline garment inventory management, optimize stock control, and enhance operational efficiency.</p></p>
