<p><p>The Genetically Modified Food Safety Testing Market includes various methodologies, primarily Immunoassay and Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR). Immunoassays are used to detect specific proteins or antibodies associated with genetically modified organisms (GMOs), providing rapid results. In contrast, PCR is a molecular technique that amplifies DNA sequences, enabling the identification of GMO genes with high sensitivity and specificity. Both methods are crucial for ensuring food safety and regulatory compliance, facilitating the traceability and labeling of genetically modified products in the food supply chain.</p></p>
