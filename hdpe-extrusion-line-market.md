<p><strong>The HDPE Extrusion Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE extrusion line market is segmented into single-layer and multilayer types. Single-layer extrusion involves producing a uniform layer of high-density polyethylene, ideal for applications requiring simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multilayer extrusion combines different materials or varying HDPE properties, enhancing barrier functions, strength, and versatility for specialized applications such as food packaging and industrial containers. The choice between single-layer and multilayer systems depends on the specific requirements of product performance and design.</p></p>
