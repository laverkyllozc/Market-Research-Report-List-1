<p><p>Industrial Emergency Shutdown Systems (ESD) encompass various components ensuring safety in hazardous environments. Safety controllers, modules, and relays manage emergency procedures. Safety switches detect and initiate shutdowns. Logic solvers or programmable safety systems process inputs to make decisions for safe operations. Emergency stop devices provide immediate manual shutdown capabilities. Actuators facilitate physical shutdown actions, while safety sensors monitor conditions to trigger responses. Valves control the flow of hazardous materials, ensuring containment during emergencies, thus vital for industrial safety.</p></p>
