<p><strong>The Liquid Biopsy Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CTCs</li><li>ctDNA</li><li>Exosomes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid biopsy products market comprises three main types: circulating tumor cells (CTCs), circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA), and exosomes. CTCs are intact cancer cells shed into the bloodstream, providing insights into tumor characteristics. ctDNA consists of fragmented DNA released from tumors, useful for genetic profiling and monitoring treatment response. Exosomes are nano-sized vesicles that carry proteins and RNA from tumors, playing a role in intercellular communication and potential biomarkers for various cancers. Together, they enhance non-invasive cancer diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>
