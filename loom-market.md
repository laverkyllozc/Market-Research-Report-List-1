<p><strong>The Loom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Jet Loom</li><li>Water Jet Loom</li><li>Rapier and Projectile Loom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Loom market types include Air Jet, Water Jet, Rapier, and Projectile looms, each designed for specific textile manufacturing needs. Air Jet looms utilize compressed air for yarn propulsion, offering high-speed production and efficiency. Water Jet looms use water to transport the yarn, ideal for synthetic fabrics but not suitable for water-sensitive materials. Rapier looms employ a gripper mechanism to handle yarn, providing versatility in fabric types. Projectile looms use a small projectile to transfer the weft, suitable for heavy fabrics and wider weaving.</p></p>
