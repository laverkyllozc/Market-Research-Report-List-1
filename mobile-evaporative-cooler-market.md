<p><ul><li>Up-wind</li><li>Down-wind</li><li>Side-wind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile evaporative cooler market can be categorized into three types based on airflow direction: up-wind, down-wind, and side-wind. Up-wind coolers draw air from behind and blow it forward, suitable for larger spaces. Down-wind coolers release cooled air downward, effectively cooling specific areas, often used in commercial applications. Side-wind coolers intake air from the sides, ideal for narrow spaces or corridors. Each type serves distinct environments, optimizing cooling efficiency based on air movement patterns.</p></p>
