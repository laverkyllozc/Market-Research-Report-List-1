<p><p>The optical coupler equipment market is primarily divided into two types: linear optocouplers and non-linear optocouplers. Linear optocouplers are designed for applications requiring precise signal transmission and are often utilized in analog signal processing, maintaining linearity in voltage or current signals. Conversely, non-linear optocouplers cater to digital signal transmissions and are commonly found in applications like data communication and control systems, where precise linearity is less critical, but speed and integration capability are essential.</p></p>
