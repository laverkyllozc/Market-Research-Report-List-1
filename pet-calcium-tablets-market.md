<p><p>The pet calcium tablets market is divided into two main types: natural calcium agents and artificial calcium agents. Natural calcium agents are derived from organic sources such as fish bones, shellfish, or algae, providing essential minerals in a form that is easily digestible for pets. In contrast, artificial calcium agents are synthesized or chemically manufactured compounds, designed to offer precise dosages of calcium. Each type serves specific dietary needs, influencing choices based on pet health requirements and owner preferences.</p></p>
