<p><p>The pharmaceutical cleaning validation market involves ensuring that cleaning processes effectively remove residues from equipment and surfaces, crucial for product safety and compliance. Direct sampling involves taking physical samples from surfaces to analyze for contaminants, providing immediate verification of cleaning effectiveness. In contrast, indirect sampling assesses the effectiveness of cleaning through environmental monitoring and testing equipment or air, indirectly inferring cleanliness. Both methods are essential for ensuring pharmaceutical products meet stringent regulatory standards and maintain patient safety.</p></p>
