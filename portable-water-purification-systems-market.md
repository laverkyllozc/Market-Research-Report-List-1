<p><strong>The Portable Water Purification Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange</li><li>Filtration</li><li>Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable water purification systems market includes several key types: ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. Ion exchange systems use resin beads to remove impurities by swapping ions, effectively softening water. Filtration systems utilize physical barriers, such as membranes or activated carbon, to eliminate contaminants and particles. Distillation involves boiling water to create vapor and then condensing it back into liquid, effectively isolating impurities. Each type offers unique advantages, making them suitable for varying portable water purification needs.</p></p>
