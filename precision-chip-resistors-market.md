<p><p>The precision chip resistors market is primarily segmented into thin film and thick film types. Thin film resistors are manufactured using a process that deposits a very thin layer of resistive material, offering high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring low noise and high precision. In contrast, thick film resistors are fabricated from a thicker layer of resistive materials, providing robustness and cost-effectiveness, making them more popular in consumer electronics and general-purpose applications.</p></p>
