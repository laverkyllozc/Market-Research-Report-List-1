<p><ul><li>Centerbulb Type</li><li>Dumbbell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC waterstop market is divided into two main types: Centerbulb Type and Dumbbell Type. The Centerbulb Type features a central bulb that enhances water retention and provides effective sealing in construction joints, ideal for basement and underground structures. Conversely, the Dumbbell Type has a tapered design that allows for easier installation in various joint configurations, making it versatile for different applications. Both types serve crucial roles in preventing water ingress in concrete structures, ensuring durability and integrity in construction projects.</p></p>
