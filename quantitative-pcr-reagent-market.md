<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantitative PCR (qPCR) reagent market is primarily segmented into dye-based and probe-based reagents. Dye-based reagents use fluorescent dyes, such as SYBR Green, which bind to double-stranded DNA, allowing quantification through fluorescence intensity. In contrast, probe-based reagents rely on specific probes, such as TaqMan or molecular beacons, which emit fluorescence upon hybridization to target DNA sequences. Both types facilitate the detection and quantification of nucleic acids, essential for applications in research, diagnostics, and clinical testing.</p></p>
