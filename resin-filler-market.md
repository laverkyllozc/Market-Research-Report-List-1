<p><ul><li>Glass Filler</li><li>Inorganic Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin filler market is categorized into two main types: glass fillers and inorganic fillers. Glass fillers are typically used to enhance the mechanical properties and thermal stability of resins, providing improved strength and durability. They are commonly utilized in applications such as automotive and construction. Inorganic fillers, on the other hand, consist of materials like calcium carbonate and talc, which are used to reduce costs, enhance fire resistance, and improve dimensional stability in various resin applications.</p></p>
