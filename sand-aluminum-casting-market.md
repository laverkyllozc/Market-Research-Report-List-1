<p><strong>The Sand Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Green Sand</li><li>Dry Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sand aluminum casting market is primarily categorized into two types: green sand and dry sand. Green sand casting utilizes a mixture of sand, clay, and water, enabling excellent moldability and thermal conductivity. This method is cost-effective for high-volume production. In contrast, dry sand casting employs a mixture of sand and a binding agent without moisture, resulting in greater strength and durability, suited for intricate designs and larger castings. Both methods cater to various industries, enhancing casting quality and efficiency.</p></p>
