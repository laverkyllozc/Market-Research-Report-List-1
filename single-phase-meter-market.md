<p><p>The single-phase meter market consists of two primary types: electromechanical induction meters and electronic energy meters. Electromechanical induction meters use a spinning disc mechanism driven by electromagnetic induction to measure energy consumption, offering reliability in simpler applications. Conversely, electronic energy meters utilize digital technology for accurate measurement and enhanced features like programmable settings and data storage. These meters cater to various energy management needs, with electronic versions increasingly preferred for their precision and advanced functionality in modern energy systems.</p></p>
