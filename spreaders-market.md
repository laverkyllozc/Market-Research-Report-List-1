<p><p>The spreaders market is segmented into three main types: electric spreaders, electro-hydraulic spreaders, and pneumatic spreaders. Electric spreaders utilize electric motors for efficient and precise material distribution, ideal for smaller applications. Electro-hydraulic spreaders combine hydraulic systems with electric controls for enhanced power and versatility, suitable for larger projects. Pneumatic spreaders rely on compressed air for material transport, offering rapid deployment and uniform spread patterns, making them effective in various industrial and agricultural settings.</p></p>
