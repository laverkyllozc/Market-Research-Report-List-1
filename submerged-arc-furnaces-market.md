<p><p>The submerged arc furnace (SAF) market primarily consists of two types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) submerged arc furnaces. DC submerged arc furnaces are favored for their efficient energy consumption and precise control over the smelting process, making them suitable for high-purity metal production. In contrast, AC submerged arc furnaces are widely used for their robust design and cost-effectiveness, serving various industries such as ferroalloy production and mineral processing. Each type has distinct advantages tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
