<p><p>The Telehealth market can be categorized into three types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise. Web-based telehealth solutions are accessed through browsers and require minimal setup, providing flexibility for users. Cloud-based systems store data on external servers, facilitating easy access and scalability while reducing infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premise telehealth solutions are installed locally on a healthcare provider's infrastructure, offering greater control and security but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Each type serves varying organizational needs.</p></p>
