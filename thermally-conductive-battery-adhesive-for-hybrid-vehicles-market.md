<p><p>Thermally conductive battery adhesives for hybrid vehicles are essential for ensuring effective heat management and structural integrity within battery packs. The market is divided into two main types: gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers provide thermal conductivity between battery components, bridging spaces while optimizing heat dissipation. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, offer both thermal properties and mechanical bonding, enhancing the overall strength and durability of battery assemblies, which is crucial for the performance and safety of hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
