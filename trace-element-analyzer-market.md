<p><p>The trace element analyzer market encompasses various technologies for detecting and quantifying trace elements. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) utilizes light absorption principles to measure metal concentrations, making it ideal for precise elemental analysis in various samples. On the other hand, electrochemical analysis involves measuring electrical signals to assess the presence of specific trace elements, providing high sensitivity and selectivity. Both methods are widely employed in environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, food safety, and industrial applications, ensuring accurate trace element evaluation.</p></p>
