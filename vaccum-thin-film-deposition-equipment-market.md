<p><p>The vacuum thin film deposition equipment market comprises three main types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD), and Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD). PVD involves the physical transfer of material to create thin films, often for applications like coatings and semiconductors. CVD, on the other hand, uses chemical reactions to produce films, suitable for complex shapes and materials. ALD offers precise control over film thickness at the atomic level, enabling high-quality coatings for advanced electronics and nanotechnology.</p></p>
