<p><p>The Vehicle-Mounted Auxiliary Converters market is primarily segmented into two types: DC Level Output and AC Level Output. DC Level Output converters provide direct current for various applications, such as powering electronic devices or charging batteries within vehicles, ensuring efficient energy use. In contrast, AC Level Output converters convert direct current into alternating current, enabling the operation of standard electrical equipment and appliances. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle functionality and supporting modern auxiliary systems.</p></p>
