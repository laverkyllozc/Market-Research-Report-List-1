<p><p>Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology enables electric vehicles (EVs) to interact with the power grid. Unidirectional V2G involves EVs sourcing energy from the grid for charging without exporting energy back, primarily focusing on peak power management. In contrast, Bidirectional V2G allows EVs to not only draw power but also discharge surplus energy back to the grid, enhancing grid stability and supporting renewable energy integration. Both market types play essential roles in optimizing energy use and enhancing grid resilience.</p></p>
